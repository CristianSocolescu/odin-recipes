Best Whiskey Sour

The whiskey sour (aka bourbon sour) is a classic cocktail that’s on the list of International Bartender Association’s IBA official cocktails.

This means it has an official definition. Our version stays true to the original definition, with a few tweaks.

The ingredients for making a whiskey sour recipe are simple! Here’s what you’ll need for this classic sour cocktail:

Whiskey sour ingredients

  • Bourbon whiskey
  • Lemon juice
  • Maple Syrup

These are the few steps you should follow when making an whiskey sour cocktail

  1. Shake up ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice
  2. Add a silce of orange and a cocktail cherry
